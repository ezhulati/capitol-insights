CAPITOL-INSIGHTS TEAM

Our mission is to serve a broad spectrum of clients including telecommunications, local municipalities, transportation, information technology, and private law enforcement at the federal, state and local levels. Our clients rely on us to communicate design and communicate complex strategies to key policy makers, legislators, and government leaders to achieve their objectives.

Drew CampbellPresident

Drew Campbell has thirty years of lobbying experience that included working for the Associated Motor Carriers of Oklahoma and culminated in a twenty five year stint with the New Car Dealers Association of Metropolitan Dallas, Inc as its CEO. During his tenure with the associations, he forged countless relationships with business leaders and political figures at the local, State and Federal levels. Along with legislative management, Drew developed a firm grasp of media and public relations skills as he successfully represented the automobile industry as its regional and national spokesperson during his stint as CEO of the Association. Since opening Capitol Insights, Drew has brought his many skills to represent a variety of clients and business interests, including major financial, transportation and manufacturing interests.

BYRON CampbellSenior Partner

Byron has over ten years experience in the political and government arena on the local, state and federal levels. Starting his career in DC after graduating from University of North Texas with a political science degree, Byron worked in every aspect of the governmental process. From running a Congressional campaign to directing a non-profit board, Byron has found success in each of his professional endeavors. In 2010 Byron launched his own political and legislative consulting practice where his clientele spans the gamut of the industry spectrum.
