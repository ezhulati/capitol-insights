89th Regular Legislative Session

January 14, 2025 - May 26, 2025

Introduction

The 89th Texas Legislature convenes on January 14, 2025, for its regular 140-day session, concluding on May 26, 2025. This calendar provides a comprehensive overview of key dates, deadlines, and events during the legislative session to help organizations and individuals effectively plan their legislative engagement strategies.

The Texas Legislature meets in regular session every two years, making this concentrated period critical for advancing policy priorities. Understanding the legislative timeline is essential for effective advocacy, as specific deadlines determine when bills must be filed, heard in committee, and passed by each chamber.

This calendar highlights:

  • Constitutional deadlines for legislative action
  • Key procedural dates in both the House and Senate
  • Committee hearing schedules and deadlines
  • Recess periods and state holidays
  • Strategic planning considerations for advocates

Use this calendar to develop a structured approach to legislative engagement, ensuring that advocacy efforts align with the procedural rhythm of the session. As each legislative session has unique dynamics, these dates serve as guideposts within the broader context of the legislative process.

Key Dates & Deadlines

The following table outlines the most critical dates during the 89th Legislative Session. These constitutional and procedural deadlines determine the rhythm of legislative activity and create important checkpoints for bill progression.

Date Day Description
January 14, 2025 Tuesday 89th Legislature convenes at noon (First day)
March 7, 2025 Friday 60th day - Last day to file bills and joint resolutions without suspension of rules
May 12, 2025 Monday 119th day - Last day for House committees to report House bills and joint resolutions
May 15, 2025 Thursday 122nd day - Last day for House to consider second reading House bills and joint resolutions
May 17, 2025 Saturday 124th day - Last day for House to consider local House bills on third reading
May 20, 2025 Tuesday 127th day - Last day for House committees to report Senate bills and joint resolutions
May 22, 2025 Thursday 129th day - Last day for House to consider second reading Senate bills and joint resolutions
May 23, 2025 Friday 130th day - Last day for House to consider Senate bills and joint resolutions on third reading
May 25, 2025 Sunday 132nd day - Last day for House to act on Senate amendments
May 26, 2025 Monday 133rd day - Last day for Senate to concur in House amendments or adopt conference committee reports; Last day of 89th Regular Session (Sine Die)
June 15, 2025 Sunday 20th day following final adjournment - Last day Governor can sign or veto bills passed during the regular session
September 1, 2025 Monday 91st day following final adjournment - Date that bills without specific effective dates become law

Note: The above dates are based on the projected schedule for the 89th Legislature. The legislative leadership may adjust certain procedural deadlines, and additional deadlines may be established in the House and Senate rules adopted at the beginning of the session.
